
    Main result
    ===========

    The full grid search tested 8 configurations.
    The adaptive benchmark tested 5 configurations.

    Best (grid search)    : n_jobs=7  -> 6.698 s
    Best (adaptive search): n_jobs=6  -> 9.149 s

    Accuracy: 0.948

    Conclusion:
    The adaptive benchmark found the same optimum with 3 fewer tests.
    The fitted parallel fraction p = 86% shows that most of the
    computation is parallelisable, but Amdahl's Law still limits
    the maximum achievable speedup.
