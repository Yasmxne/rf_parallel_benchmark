
    Main result
    ===========

    The full grid search tested 8 configurations.
    The adaptive benchmark tested 5 configurations.

    The best configuration found by grid search is n_jobs=8
    with a training time of 4.262 seconds.

    The best configuration found by adaptive search is n_jobs=8
    with a training time of 4.280 seconds.

    Both methods obtained an accuracy of approximately 0.948.

    Conclusion:
    The adaptive benchmark found a similar best configuration while using fewer tests.
    This shows that it can reduce the benchmarking cost compared to a full grid search.

    The speedup curve also shows that the gain is not perfectly linear.
    Most of the improvement happens when moving from 1 worker to several workers,
    then the additional gains become smaller.
